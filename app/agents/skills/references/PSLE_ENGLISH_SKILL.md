# PSLE 英语专项技能 (SKILL.md)

## 1. 核心题型与解题策略 (Core Components & Strategies)

### A. Situational Writing (SW)
- **PAC Framework**: 
  - **P**urpose: Why are you writing? (e.g., to invite, to complain).
  - **A**udience: Who are you writing to? Dictates Formal vs Informal tone.
  - **C**ontext: What is the situation?
- **Tone & Format**:
  - Formal (to principal/manager): No contractions (e.g., do not instead of don't), polite expressions, formal sign-off (Yours sincerely/faithfully).
  - Informal (to friend/family): Contractions allowed, warm sign-off (Best regards, Love).
- **Golden Rule**: Include ALL 6 points given in the prompt. Do not add unnecessary information.

### B. Continuous Writing (Composition)
- **Structure**: Introduction (Hook) -> Rising Action -> Climax -> Falling Action -> Conclusion.
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Instead of "He was scared", use "His hands trembled and a cold sweat broke out on his forehead."
- **Vocabulary**: Use precise verbs and descriptive adjectives (e.g., 'sprinted' instead of 'ran fast').
- **Relevance**: The story MUST explicitly tie back to the given topic and at least one picture.

### C. Comprehension (Open-Ended & Cloze)
- **True/False/Not Given**: Must quote the *exact* phrase/sentence from the passage as evidence.
- **Inferential Questions**: "Why did the character do X?" -> Requires understanding the character's feelings or motives, not just lifting from the text.
- **Cloze Passage**: Read the whole paragraph first to understand the context. Look for clues (synonyms, antonymms, collocations) before and after the blank.

### D. Synthesis & Transformation (S&T)
- **Reported Speech**: Change tense (backshift), change pronouns, change time/place words (e.g., 'tomorrow' -> 'the next day', 'here' -> 'there').
- **Active to Passive**: Swap subject and object. Ensure tense remains the same.
- **No Change in Meaning**: Ensure the original meaning is perfectly preserved. Check subject-verb agreement!

## 2. 答题步骤规范 (Standard Operating Procedure)
1. **Identify the Rule/Component**: Which grammar rule or writing technique is being tested?
2. **Apply the Strategy**: Use PAC for SW, grammar rules for S&T, or "Show, Don't Tell" for composition.
3. **Verify**: For S&T, check tense and subject-verb agreement. For Comprehension, check if the answer directly addresses the question word (Who, What, Why, How).
