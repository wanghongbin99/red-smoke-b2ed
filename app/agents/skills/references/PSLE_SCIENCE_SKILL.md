# PSLE 科学专项指令

## 0. 科学思维能力要求 (Scientific Thinking Skills)
Science isn't just about facts; it's about thinking like a scientist. The exam tests these skills:
- **Observing & Comparing**: Noting similarities and differences.
- **Analyzing**: Interpreting data from tables, graphs, and diagrams.
- **Inferring & Predicting**: Making logical conclusions and forecasts.
- **Evaluating**: Designing fair tests and identifying variables (changed, measured, constant).

## 1. 核心主题知识库 (Based on Curriculum Map)

根据图片章节，当涉及以下主题时，必须激活相应的科学原理：

### A. Diversity (多样性) - P3/P4 基础

- **考点**: Living/Non-living things, Materials (Strength, Flexibility, Waterproof).
- **指令**: 比较不同材料的属性时，必须使用 "Comparison terms" (e.g., "more flexible than", "the most waterproof").

### B. Cycles (循环) - P4/P5/P6

- **考点**: Life Cycles, Reproduction (Plants/Animals), Water Cycle (Matter).
- **指令**:
  - **Water Cycle**: 必须区分 "Evaporation" (heat gain) 和 "Condensation" (heat loss)。
  - **Matter**: 强调体积 (Volume) 和形状 (Shape) 的固定性。

### C. Systems (系统) - P5/P6

- **考点**: Plant/Human Systems (Respiratory, Circulatory), Electrical Systems.
- **指令**:
  - **Human Systems**: 说明各系统如何 "Work together"（如：循环系统运输呼吸系统吸收的氧气）。
  - **Electrical**: 区分 Series vs Parallel，解释 "Closed circuit" 和 "Gap in the circuit"。

### D. Energy & Interactions (能量与相互作用) - P6 高频

- **考点**: Photosynthesis, Energy Conversion, Forces (Magnetic, Frictional, Gravitational), Environment.
- **指令**:
  - **Energy**: 必须写出能量转换链（e.g., Potential Energy -> Kinetic Energy -> Sound Energy）。
  - **Forces**: 区分 "Contact forces" 和 "Non-contact forces"。

## 2. 强制答题框架 (The CER Method)

所有开放性问题 (OEQ) 的回答必须遵循此结构：

1. **C (Claim)**: 直接回答问题（是、否、增加、减少）。
2. **E (Evidence)**: 引用题目图表或实验中的观测数据/现象。
3. **R (Reasoning)**: 运用科学原理（如：Heat gain/loss, Expansion/Contraction）解释证据为何支持结论。

## 3. 关键词避坑指南 (Common Pitfalls)

- **Heat**: 严禁说 "Coldness enters"，必须说 "Heat is lost to the surroundings"。
- **Light**: 强调 "Light is reflected by the object into our eyes"。
- **Plant Transport**: 明确区分 Xylem (water/mineral salts) 和 Phloem (food/sugar)。
- **Photosynthesis**: 必须提到必备条件：Light, Chlorophyll, Water, Carbon Dioxide。

## 4. 示例交互

**输入**: "为什么在热水杯上盖个盖子，盖子内侧会出现水珠？"
**输出 (符合 SKILL 规范)**:

- **主题识别**: Cycles (Water Cycle - Condensation).
- **CER 解析**:
  - [Evidence]: 热水蒸发成水蒸气 (Water vapour)。
  - [Reasoning]: 水蒸气上升接触到较冷的盖子内表面，**失去热量 (Loses heat)** 并 **凝结 (Condenses)** 成水滴。
# PSLE 科学专项：章节分析与满分词汇表

## 主题 1：Diversity (多样性)
- **章节分析**: P3-P4 基础，侧重分类与材料属性。
- **满分词汇表**:
  - **Materials**: Waterproof, Flexible, Strong, Transparent, Buoyancy (浮力).
  - **Fungi/Bacteria**: Decomposers (分解者), Spores (孢子), Need moisture/warmth to grow.
- **避坑**: 描述材料时，不说 "don't break"，要说 "Strong/High tensile strength"。

## 主题 2：Cycles (循环)
- **章节分析**: 涵盖生命周期与物质状态变化。
- **满分词汇表**:
  - **Life Cycles**: Larva, Pupa, Nymph, Moulting (蜕皮), Competitive/Non-competitive.
  - **Matter/Water**: **Heat gain** (吸热), **Heat loss** (放热), **Condense** (凝结), **Evaporate** (蒸发), Rate of evaporation, Melting point.
- **核心句式**: "Water vapour **gained heat** from the surroundings and **evaporated** into water vapour."

## 主题 3：Systems (系统)
- **章节分析**: 人体系统与电路系统（P5/P6 重点）。
- **满分词汇表**:
  - **Digestive**: Digested food, Absorbed into the bloodstream, Small intestine.
  - **Respiratory**: Gaseous exchange, Surface area, Air sacs (Alveoli).
  - **Circulatory**: Transport oxygen/rich blood, Heart pumps blood to all parts of the body.
  - **Electrical**: **Closed circuit**, **Gap**, **Electrical conductor/insulator**, **Parallel/Series arrangement**.
- **必考逻辑**: 说明各系统如何 **"Work together"** 以维持生命活动。

## 主题 4：Interactions (相互作用)
- **章节分析**: 力学、磁铁与环境。
- **满分词汇表**:
  - **Forces**: Frictional force (摩擦力), Gravitational force (重力), Elastic spring force.
  - **Magnets**: Like poles **repel**, Unlike poles **attract**, Magnetic materials (Iron, Steel, Nickel, Cobalt).
  - **Environment**: Global warming, Greenhouse effect, Deforestation, Predators vs Prey.
- **核心句式**: "Frictional force between [Surface A] and [Surface B] **opposes motion**."

## 主题 5：Energy (能量)
- **章节分析**: 光、热、能量转化与光合作用。
- **满分词汇表**:
  - **Photosynthesis**: Chlorophyll, Light energy -> Chemical potential energy, Oxygen as a by-product.
  - **Light**: Reflection (反射), Refraction (折射), Opaque/Transparent/Translucent, Shadow formation.
  - **Heat**: **Good/Poor conductor of heat**, Expand (膨胀), Contract (收缩).
- **能量转化链**: "Gravitational potential energy -> Kinetic energy -> Sound + Heat energy."
